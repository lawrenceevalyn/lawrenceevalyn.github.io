# Lawrence Evalyn

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the University of Toronto English department. My interests lie at the intersection of the digital humanities and the late eighteenth century. My dissertation examines all works printed in England between 1789 and 1799.

I recieved my Master's in English from the University of Victoria in British Columbia in 2014.

I recieved my Bachelor's in English from Duke University in Durham, North Carolina in 2012.
